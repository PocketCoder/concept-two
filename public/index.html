hello there.

My name is Jake Williams and I'm a 21 year old filmmaker from Norwich. Fresh out of the University of Winchester, I'm eager to get into the industry to start making my mark in the world of film. I've worked as a freelance videographer since 2018 and like to write in my own time, occasionally submitting my work to competitions in the hope of kickstarting my career. As an aspiring Writer/Director, I want to make films that tell stories often left unheard, giving a voice to those overlooked by mainstream cinema.
